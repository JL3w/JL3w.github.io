About Me

Hi, I'm Joshua! I grew up in Coastal North Carolina and consider the beach my home. My love for computing began at an early age playing Anti Ballistic Missle for DOS on my grandfather's PC. In highschool percussion, tennis, and Roller Hockey were my main extracurriculars . Upon graduating from NCSU I started my internship at the NC Ferry Division

From there I moved on to a career in IT focusing on developing and implementing workflows for electronic documents, and Networking, specifically large wireless deployments. While I enjoyed IT, my aspiriations grew and I joined the MetricWise team.
